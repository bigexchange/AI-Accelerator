When to Use Claude, ChatGPT, or Gemini

All three are good. None of them is best at everything. The decision rules — with a side-by-side table and what most people get wrong.

🧭
What you'll have
A clear set of rules for which AI tool to reach for, so you stop defaulting to one tool for everything and start getting better results faster.

G
Gemini
Default rule: if you're already in a Google tool and the task takes under 5 minutes, don't open a new tab.
Google Workspace
Use when
📧 You're in Gmail and need a fast summary
A 40-email thread, a back-and-forth with procurement, a customer escalation chain. Gemini reads it and gives you the TLDR without leaving your inbox.
"Summarize my thread with [contact] about [subject]. What decisions were made, what's still open, and what do I owe them?"
📝 You're in Google Docs and need to write or rewrite
The Gemini sidebar is right there. Ask it to make a section more concise, rewrite in active voice, or add a missing section — it edits in place.
"Make this section more direct. Lead with the business impact. Cut anything that's filler."
📊 You're in Google Sheets and need formulas or analysis
Ask what you need in plain English — it writes the formula. No Excel expertise required.
"Which reps have the highest win rate this quarter? Flag any row where the close date has passed."
🗒️ Notebook LM — researching a document collection
Upload competitive intel, customer transcripts, or analyst reports. Notebook LM builds a knowledge base you can chat with. Cites everything. Genuinely underused.
BigID: upload competitive intel + ask "what are our strongest differentiators against Cyera?" · Upload customer call transcripts + ask "what pain points come up most?"
C
Claude
Default rule: when quality, complexity, or connections to other tools matter — use Claude.
Complex · Connected · High-stakes
Use when
✍️ The output needs to be genuinely good
Not a rough draft you'll rewrite — something you'd actually send. A proposal, press release, executive brief, QBR deck. Claude produces output that sounds like a skilled human wrote it.
🔌 You need to connect to Salesforce, Gmail, Drive, or Slack
With MCP connectors enabled, Claude can pull a Salesforce account, check your Gmail, look at your calendar, and search your Drive — all in one conversation.
"I have a call with [Company] tomorrow. Pull context from Salesforce, check my recent emails with them, and write me a one-page prep brief."
📚 The task is long, complex, or multi-part
Claude handles enormous context — a full RFP, a 50-page contract, a complete sales deck — and can work with all of it at once without losing the thread.
🔁 You need consistent output across many sessions
Claude Projects let you bake in your instructions, voice, and documents. Every conversation starts from the same foundation — perfect for always-on brand voice or recurring analysis.
🏷️ You need a branded BigID document
PDFs and slide decks with proper BigID branding. Gemini and ChatGPT will write the content but won't format it to BigID brand standards. Claude will.
AI
ChatGPT
Default rule: you have a data file to analyze or a team tool to build and share.
Data · Team tools
Use when
📈 You have a CSV or spreadsheet and need instant analysis
ChatGPT's code interpreter runs Python on your file in real time. Charts, pivot summaries, trend analysis — fastest path from raw data to insight.
"Analyze this pipeline CSV. Show win rate by segment, top 10 deals by value, and a 6-month trend. Create a chart for each."
🛠️ You want a reusable team tool with one shareable link
Custom GPTs are shareable as a standalone URL. Build a Sales Enablement GPT with your pitch deck, competitive positioning, and objection handling baked in. Share one link with your whole team.
Good BigID GPTs: Sales Coach (pitch + rebuttals) · Content Writer (brand voice) · Proposal Generator (templates + pricing)

What most people get wrong

⚠️
Using only one tool for everythingThe people who get the most done use all three for what each is actually best at. Claude users try to make it do everything. So do ChatGPT users. Don't.
⚠️
Not using Gemini in contextMost people open gemini.google.com in a new tab when Gemini is already sitting in the sidebar of Gmail, Docs, and Sheets. The fastest version is the one already open next to your doc.
⚠️
Not using Notebook LMIf you have a collection of documents you reference repeatedly — competitive intel, customer research, analyst reports — Notebook LM turns it into something you can talk to. Wildly underused.
⚠️
Not building a Claude Project for recurring workEvery time you paste the same brand guide or competitive one-pager into a new Claude chat, you're doing unnecessary work. Build the Project once. Done.
