Create a Branded PDF

Two PDF skills for every document — from clean one-pagers to executive proposals with a bold header. No design tools, no templates to manage. Paste your content, get a branded file.

📄
What you'll have
A downloadable BigID PDF — BD Megatoya fonts, the purple→pink→orange gradient, BigID logo, and a gradient footer on every page. Generated in minutes.

Two skills — pick the right one

Standard
PDF Formatter
Clean professional layout. Gradient title at the top, standard body below.

Best for:

  • Reports and competitive analyses
  • One-pagers and product briefs
  • Guides, whitepapers, internal memos
Trigger phrases

"Make me a branded BigID PDF" · "Format this as a BigID report" · "Turn this into a BigID one-pager"

Executive
Dark Header
Bold color band across the top ~30% of page 1 with executive summary baked in.

Best for:

  • Executive summaries and C-suite proposals
  • Sales leave-behinds for senior stakeholders
  • Board-level reports and analyst briefs
Trigger phrases

"BigID PDF with a dark header" · "executive summary PDF" · "header band treatment"

Skill 1: PDF Formatter

Basic trigger Create a branded BigID PDF of this content. Title: "[your title]" [paste your content here]
Competitive analysis Create a branded BigID PDF of this competitive analysis. Title: "BigID vs. Cyera: A Direct Comparison." [paste content]

Skill 2: Dark Header

Comes in three themes. Claude will ask which one you want — or specify it in your prompt.

🖤 Charcoal
Deep dark bg, white text. Executive, bold, high-stakes.
🤍 Pearl
Light grey bg, dark text. Clean, editorial, versatile.
💜 Lavender
Soft purple tint, dark text. Brand-forward, warm.
Executive summary — charcoal Create a BigID executive summary PDF using the charcoal header. Title: "State of AI Data Risk 2026." Executive summary: [paste 2–3 sentences]. Then format the full report: [paste content]
Sales leave-behind — lavender I need a leave-behind for a CISO meeting. Use the dark header skill — lavender theme. Title: "Why BigID for AI Security." Executive summary: [2 sentences]. [your content sections]

Pro tips

📋
Paste your content directlyNo need to pre-format. Raw text, section by section — Claude applies the correct styling automatically.
🎯
Give Claude your actual title"BigID vs. Purview: The 2026 DSPM Comparison" is infinitely better input than "competitive comparison."
🏢
When to use Dark Header vs. FormatterGoing to C-level, in a proposal, or in a leadership meeting → Dark Header. Internal, functional, or operational → PDF Formatter.
