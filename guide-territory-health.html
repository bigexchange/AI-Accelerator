Activity health checkPull all open opportunities in the [Area] territory from Salesforce. Flag every deal that has: no activity in the last 30 days, no Deal Next Step logged, a close date already in the past, or a MEDDPICC score below 25%. Pro tips

📋
Run it before every leadership conversationWalking into a QBR without a current territory snapshot is like showing up to a customer call without doing your research.
📊
Separate vanity pipeline from real pipelineTotal pipeline looks impressive. Weighted pipeline tells you what you can actually close. Always lead with weighted, and flag inflated outliers.
🔍
The stale deal count is your territory's pulseMore than any other metric, deals with no activity in the last 30 days tells you whether a territory is being actively managed or whether reps are carrying wish lists.
⚠️
Federal pipeline is inflated by placeholdersFederal's number includes a single Stage 0 placeholder at $1B+. Always strip out outlier amounts before drawing conclusions about Federal pipeline health.
